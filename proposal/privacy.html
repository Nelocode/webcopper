Privacy Policy

Information Collection: Copper Giant Resources Corp. is committed to protecting your privacy. Any personal information collected through our newsletter subscription, form submission, or direct contact is held in the strictest confidence. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to third parties.

Use of Data: The email address and name you provide may be used to send you news releases, financial reports, or other corporate updates. If at any time you wish to unsubscribe, you can do so by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any email we send you.

Cookies & Tracking: Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small files stored on your computer that allow us to analyze traffic trends and optimize web performance. No personal data is collected or linked to these cookies.

Compliance: We comply with all applicable local, national, and international privacy laws, including the Canadian Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA).

Copper Giant, part of the Fiore Group, is dedicated to advancing high-quality copper projects responsibly. Led by a team experienced in building major mines from discovery to construction, our current focus is the Mocoa copper-molybdenum project in southern Colombia—one of the largest undeveloped copper systems in the Americas. Grounded in our Good Neighbor philosophy, we are committed to creating enduring value for our communities and contributing to the global energy transition.
