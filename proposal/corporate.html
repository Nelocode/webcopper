About Copper Giant

Corporate Profile

Copper Giant is part of the Fiore Group, a private and well-established Canadian organization known for building successful, high-impact companies across the natural resource sector. Copper Giant was formed with a singular focus: to advance high-quality copper projects beyond resource definition responsibly, efficiently, and with long-term positive impact.

The Company is led by a team with uncommon experience, having successfully taken some of the few major copper mines developed in the past two decades from discovery through to construction.

Copper Giant's current focus is the Mocoa copper-molybdenum deposit in southern Colombia, one of the largest undeveloped resources of its kind in the Americas. Recent exploration success has revealed potential well beyond its original footprint, highlighting Mocoa as a broader district-scale opportunity — and the catalyst for the Company's name and evolution.

Guided by the values of respect and responsibility, and grounded in its Good Neighbor philosophy, Copper Giant is committed to creating enduring values for all stakeholders and playing a meaningful role in the global energy transition.

Fiore Group & Frank Giustra

Backed by Visionaries
Fiore Group Founder

Frank Giustra

Frank Giustra is a visionary entrepreneur, financier, and philanthropist whose career has left an indelible mark on the global mining industry. He was instrumental in the formation and growth of Wheaton Precious Metals (formerly Silver Wheaton), pioneering the streaming model, founded Endeavour Mining—now one of the world's largest gold producers—and co-founded Aris Mining.

The Fiore Group, his private investment and advisory firm, incubates and scales innovative small-cap companies with billion-dollar potential. Copper Giant is a cornerstone of the Fiore portfolio, benefitting from decades of mine-building expertise.

Core Values

Operating Principles

Respect. Responsibility.
Guided by our operating philosophy: Good Neighbor

Respect

For the people, the culture, and the land. We bring it to every interaction and believe it is present in the strongest of relationships.

Responsibility

We own the full impact of what we do. That means protecting the land, culture, and trust around us. It also means advancing something that can matter far beyond us.

Good Neighbor

When we face decisions, our guidance is simple: what would a good neighbor do? It gives each of us the freedom and responsibility to listen first, act with care, and look for ways to build strength together.

The Copper Giant Way

Respect and Responsibility are the values we hold. Good Neighbor is how we carry them into decisions. Great projects are built through trust on the ground, reputation beyond it, and the shared will to do things right before it is required. That is how we build Copper Giant.
